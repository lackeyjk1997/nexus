## THE PROBLEM

**Section Headline:**
AI sales tools are islands. Your team isn't.

**Body:**
Today's AI sales tools automate in silos. The BDR's sequencing tool doesn't know what the SA needs on technical calls. The AE's CRM doesn't learn from what CSMs discover during onboarding. Every handoff loses context. Every new hire starts from zero.

The result: your AI gets faster at the wrong things. Reps send more emails, but SAs still walk into calls blind. Deals close faster, but CSMs inherit implementation surprises. The tools optimize locally while the organization underperforms globally.

**The real problem isn't automation. It's disconnection.**

## THE VISION

**Section Headline:**
What if your AI agents taught each other?

**Body:**
Nexus is built on a simple insight: in a great sales org, every role makes every other role better. The SA's technical depth sharpens the AE's discovery. The CSM's implementation experience reshapes how BDRs qualify. The manager's pattern recognition elevates the entire team.

Nexus encodes this into software. Every person in your sales cycle — AE, BDR, SA, CSM, manager — gets their own AI agent that handles execution: research, drafting, logging, scoring, routing. But these agents aren't isolated. They're connected through an **organizational learning loop**.

When your SA says "I keep getting on calls without knowing the prospect's auth infrastructure," that feedback flows upstream. The discovery agent starts asking about auth. The BDR's pre-call brief includes it. The next SA call is 10x better — not because of a model update, but because one human's feedback reshaped the entire system.

**The organization gets smarter with every deal cycle.**

## HOW IT WORKS

**Section Headline:**
Human judgment directs. AI agents execute. The system learns.

### 1. Every role gets an agent
AEs, BDRs, SAs, CSMs, and managers each have a configurable AI agent that handles their execution workload — research, email drafting, CRM updates, call analysis, lead scoring. Configure it in plain English: "Show me the prospect's current AI vendor at the top of every brief."

### 2. Agents handle the full sales cycle
From prospecting through close, AI agents automate what can be automated while keeping humans in the loop for what requires judgment. First-touch emails get AE approval. Discovery transcripts get AI-analyzed and human-validated. Deal stages advance automatically when milestones are confirmed.

### 3. The learning loop connects everything
Any role can submit feedback that reshapes upstream agents. An SA requests more technical context → the discovery guide updates → BDR briefs improve → SA calls get better. Every piece of feedback is tracked, versioned, and measurable. Leadership sees exactly how the system evolves and what impact each change drives.

### 4. Leadership sees the whole picture
Pipeline health, forecast accuracy, rep coaching insights, and — uniquely — a System Intelligence Score that measures how much smarter your agent network has gotten over time. Watch the gap between "what SAs need" and "what they get" close in real time.

- Mobile-responsive but optimized for desktop (leadership will view this on a laptop)
